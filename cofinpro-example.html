Spacings

Type: there are two kinds of spacings, margins: mrg and paddings: pad

Dir: there is the obvious directions: top, right, bottom, left and of course none at all

Size: everything comes in four sizes: sm, md, lg, xl
sizes are based on the current font-size sm:25%, md:50%, lg:100%, xl:150%,

mrg-right-xl mrg-top-md
pad-left-xl pad-top-xl
pad-xl mrg-md

so every spacing class looks like
(pad|mrg)-(dir)-(size)
